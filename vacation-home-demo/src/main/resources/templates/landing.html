Location

Piantini is one of the most vibrant neighborhoods in Santo Domingo, the capital of the Dominican Republic. An upscale and modern neighborhood, it boasts a rich fusion of contemporary living and Caribbean charm.

Attractions

Piantini's main attractions lie in its bustling city life. Its cosmopolitan vibe presents itself in the form of towering modern architectures, green urban parks, and bustling streets, each telling a different story of the neighborhood's evolution. One notable landmark is the "Obelisco Macho del Malec√≥n," an obelisk painted by prominent Dominican artists as a tribute to the city's rich history. Another is the National Botanical Garden, just a short drive away, where you can wander around in 400 acres of tropical flora.

Malls & Supermarkets

Piantini is famous for its shopping scene. The neighborhood is home to the Blue Mall Santo Domingo, a premium shopping destination featuring high-end international brands like Louis Vuitton, Cartier, and Bvlgari. A few steps away, you'll find Agora Mall, offering a wide range of stores and a food court with international cuisine. For daily needs and groceries, there's a host of supermarkets including Nacional Supermarket and Bravo Supermarket, both offering a range of local and international products.

Beaches

While Piantini itself doesn't have a beach, it's well situated for day trips to some of the Caribbean's most stunning beaches. Boca Chica, a popular beach among locals and tourists, is about an hour's drive away. For more secluded and pristine beach experiences, you might want to venture a little further to Juan Dolio or Punta Cana.

Activities

The neighborhood is bustling with activities. You can go shopping, dine in gourmet restaurants, or explore the nightlife in one of the many bars or clubs. You might also enjoy exploring the local art scene at Bellapart Museum, or catching a live performance at the National Theatre.
